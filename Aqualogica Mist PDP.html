FAQs

Your questions, answered.

How long does the scent last?+
Up to 8 hours of lab-verified wear-time. A couple of spritzes in the morning easily carry through your day.
What does it smell like?+
Sun Kissed Vanilla - a warm gourmand of creamy vanilla and caramel with a soft floral heart, drying down to tonka & amber.
Can I spray it on my hair?+
Yes - it's a body & hair mist. It scents hair beautifully for hours without drying it out.
Is it too strong or overpowering?+
No. It's a soft, skin-like gourmand - noticeable up close but never loud. Office and everyday safe.
Is it safe for sensitive skin?+
Yes - dermatologically tested and gentle enough for daily, all-over use. Patch test first if very reactive.
How is it different from a perfume?+
It's a lighter, skin-friendly mist meant for all-over body & hair use - softer and more wearable than a concentrated perfume.
How many sprays should I use?+
2-4 spritzes on pulse points, neck and clothes. Add a light mist over hair for an all-day scent trail.
Will it stain my clothes?+
It's a fine, clear mist that dries quickly. Spray from a short distance and let it dry before dressing to be safe.
Is it travel-friendly?+
Yes - the compact bottle and fine mist make it perfect to toss in your bag for quick refreshers on the go.
Where should I store it?+
Keep it cool and away from direct sunlight to preserve the fragrance for as long as possible.

Everything about this mist

Experience Sun Kissed Vanilla+
A long-lasting body & hair mist in a warm vanilla gourmand - creamy vanilla and caramel with a soft floral heart. Light, skin-like and never overpowering, with up to 8 hours of wear.
Who is it Best for?+
Anyone who loves warm, sweet gourmand scents and wants an everyday body mist that doubles up on hair. Gentle enough for sensitive skin and great for all genders.
How to Use?+
Spritz 2-4 times on pulse points, neck & clothes from a short distance. Add a light mist over hair for an all-day scent trail. Reapply through the day as you like.
